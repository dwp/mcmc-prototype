Asking us to look at a decision again

If you think we have made the wrong decision, you can ask us to look at it again. This called a Mandatory Reconsideration. It is one of the steps to challenge and appeal a decision.

We are more likely to make a different decision if you give us:

  • new or updated information about the change
  • evidence we have not seen before

You can also ask us to look again if you think the information and evidence was correct but the decision was wrong.
